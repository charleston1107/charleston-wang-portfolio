Lego-ing Bacheng

The project Lego-ing Bacheng is an interactive web-based narrative that explores the cultural erosion and memory in Bacheng’s old town amidst rapid urban redevelopment. It merges immersive fieldwork findings – including photographs, audio, and oral histories – with creative storytelling and web technology to reanimate a disappearing local heritage. Through a multi-layered design, the project juxtaposes archival memories against present-day realities, inviting users to reflect on the tension between progress and preservation and to engage with the “spectral” traces of a vanishing community.
